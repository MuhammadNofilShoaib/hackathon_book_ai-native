# Introduction to Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

Welcome to the AI-Native Textbook for Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics. This textbook covers:

- Physical AI principles and embodied intelligence
- ROS 2 for robotic systems
- Simulation environments (Gazebo, Unity)
- NVIDIA Isaac for AI robotics
- Vision-Language-Action (VLA) systems

Each module contains 3,000-5,000 words with practical examples, exercises, and learning objectives.
